Badminton

Badminton Racket

A badminton racket is lightweight, typically made of materials like carbon fiber or aluminum for strength and flexibility. Its design includes a stringed head, a slim shaft, and a comfortable grip to provide precision and power.

Shuttlecock

The shuttlecock, or birdie, has a rounded cork base with a skirt of feathers or synthetic materials. It is designed to be aerodynamic, ensuring stability and consistent flight during play.

Badminton Net

The badminton net is stretched tightly across the center of the court, dividing it into two equal halves. It is 5 feet high at the center and is made of fine mesh to prevent the shuttlecock from passing through.
